ingredients

  • Meat - Italian Sausage and Lean Ground Beef
  • Onion - One Onion
  • Garlic - two cloves
  • Tomato Products - Can of Crushed Tomatoes, Two Cans of Tomato Sauce, and Two cans of Tomato paste
  • Sugar - Two Table Spoons White Sugar
  • Spices - Parsley, Dried Basil Leaves
  • Seasonings - Salt, Italian Seasonings, Fennel Seeds, and Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses - Parmesan, Mozzarella, and Ricotta
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
